In 2001, Don decided he would try to see all of the Major League baseball stadiums in the near future.  With that in mind, we made arrangements to see Olympic Stadium in Montreal and Toronto's SkyDome while on our Canada trip, Shea Stadium and Yankee Stadium in New York, Coors Field in Denver, Turner Field in Atlanta and Pac Bell Park (now AT&T Park) in San Francisco.  He finally got a picture at Angels Stadium in Anaheim but we don't have one at Dodger Stadium in Los Angeles, even though he's been there many times.  In 2009 he and Jeff visited the beautiful new ballpark in Phoenix and he and friend Don Rickner visited Petco Park in San Diego in June 2010.  In July, 2010 we went to Chicago and Milwaukee and saw Wrigley Field, Miller Park and US Cellular Field.  we've also been to RFK Stadium in Washington DC but cannot find the pictures.  They have a beautiful new park now so we'll have to go again and see Baltimore too.
