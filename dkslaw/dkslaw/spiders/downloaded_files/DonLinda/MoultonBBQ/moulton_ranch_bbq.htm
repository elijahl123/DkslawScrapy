In September, 2001, Ed & Carole Wong, Frank & Kathy Clarke and Don & Linda Smith decided since the community was 10 years old, it was time to get to know the neighbors.  So, they organized and hosted the first Moulton Ranch Neighborhood Barbeque which was held on Fletcher Place.  About 44 souls attended.  (Click on the dates above to see more pictures).

 For reasons beyond our control, the BBQ in 2002 was cancelled.
