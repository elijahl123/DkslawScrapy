The Year of Big Changes

Hello again from the Smith Family!!

Linda and Don are once again “empty-nesters” and that took a little getting used to.  Jennifer graduated from USC Law School in May and her husband Eric graduated from DeVry Institute in June.  Jenn took a position as an attorney in Roseland, NJ and they moved there during the summer. Eric quickly secured a position as a technology analyst for the Superior Court system in Hackensack, so they are settling in to their new life. Don has already been back to visit six times.  Jenn and Eric returned to California for Thanksgiving and Christmas, which made us very happy. 

Holly decided it was time to have a place of her own and she moved to an apartment in San Clemente in September.  We miss her, especially when we go on walks, and Cassidy and Mugsy miss Ruby.  But Ruby comes to visit sometimes which is nice.  Holly and Mike broke up and she is now dating a very nice man named Steve.  She is set to begin nurses’ training next spring so her life has seen many dramatic changes in 2001.  Since she has been successful in everything she has done, we know she will be an outstanding nurse as well.

The kids have moved farther away and Linda has found work closer to home.  Although it was hard to leave a District she loved, one of our dear friends, Peter Hartman, became ill and it was impossible to lend the type of support she wanted to and work 70 miles away.  A perfect position became available in a District with a great Superintendent, so she applied and was very surprised when Placentia-Yorba Linda offered her the job.   Her responsibilities now include all aspects of technology support including networking, pc support and information services.  It’s a steep learning curve, which means she is loving every minute.  It seems that in all the jobs she has had she loves the people she works with.  Despite the change, she and Don were able to travel to New Jersey and Atlanta, but they missed Washington, DC for the first time in several years. They hope to go to DC next February!  Because of Don’s frequent forays to visit Jenn and Eric, there was only one big vacation trip this year.  Don, Linda, Jenn and Eric set off for Ireland and Scotland in March but due to the Foot & Mouth disease problem, they ended up in Rome.  It was great fun!

Kelly, Marc, Michael, and Maddie bought a very nice home in San Clemente and they are busily fixing it to their specifications.  Marc's dad helped them put in a brand new kitchen and it looks great!!!  Kelly is still at Peoplesoft and she will probably be named Presidenhappy and easy to please, but is a normal three year old and can be a handful at times.  She visits her grandparents occasionally and they greatly enjoy their time with her.
