1999

... the last of the 90's.  What a wonderful year!!

Linda continues the ancient So. California tradition of drive, drive and then drive some more.  Her job as the Director of Technology for the San Bernardino City Unified School District has expanded to include the network, computer repair and PC support in addition to all instructional technology.  But at least they didn't burden her with higher pay.  She now has a staff of 17 (up from 7) and she is still doing presentations for various organizations.  As a result she and Don traveled to Washington DC, in February and to San Antonio and Dallas in November.  San Antonio was great fun and not just because Don was born there.  It really is a fun place to visit . Linda, Don and Jenn went to Spain from Dec. 27 to Jan. 9.  Well Linda and Don did.  Jenn returned home after 7 days while we continued on to Southern Spain.  In June Don and Linda returned to their honeymoon hotel (the Riverside Mission Inn) for their 24th wedding anniversary.  It is a beautiful hotel and close enough so Linda could return to work in the afternoon!!!  Our second annual summer trip to Las Vegas was great as usual.
